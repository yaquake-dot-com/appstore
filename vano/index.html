Native macOS music player

Listen closer to your music.

Vano Audio Player is a focused desktop player for listeners who care about their library, their metadata, and every detail in the signal path.

macOS 26 and later

A library that stays in view.

Browse folders, build a playlist, search your collection, and switch between track and album views without interrupting the music.

01

Library-first browsing

Open a music folder and explore its hierarchy directly from the sidebar. Add albums, files, or whole folders to your playlist.

02

Playback without friction

Use queue controls, shuffle, repeat, seeking, volume control, and per-device output selection in one calm workspace.

03

Made for real collections

Vano supports common music formats, embedded artwork, local covers, CUE sheets, favorites, and metadata-rich album views.

Sound control

Tune the listening chain.

A built-in equalizer provides a 10-band manual mode and searchable headphone correction presets. Vano can also match the output device sample rate to the current track and supports exclusive output mode.

10-band EQHeadphone presets Auto preampSample-rate matching Exclusive modeOutput device picker

Organize and enrich

Metadata tools that respect your files.

Edit tags and filenames, work with CUE sheets, find release information through MusicBrainz, fetch album art, or open selected files in MusicBrainz Picard. AcoustID identification helps reconnect an unknown file with its proper release.

Privacy

Your music collection stays yours.

Vano Audio Player stores its library state and preferences locally on your Mac. It does not include advertising, tracking, or analytics. Optional lookups for lyrics, MusicBrainz metadata, Cover Art Archive artwork, and AcoustID identification are made only when you choose to use those features.
