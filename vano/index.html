Built for varied collections.

From everyday compressed files to high-resolution and disc-image libraries, Vano keeps your music in one place.

Precision, built in

Start with settings made for your gear.

Vano pairs detailed playback controls with practical tools for tuning headphones and restoring the context around your music.

Headphone correction

Thousands of EQ presets, ready to search.

Choose a measured correction profile for your headphones from a library of thousands of presets. Search by model, brand, or measurement source, then fine-tune the result with Vano's parametric filters and automatic preamp control.

MusicBrainz lookup

Reconnect a file with its release.

Search MusicBrainz from the player to compare matching releases and track lists. Select the right edition, fetch its cover art, and apply accurate tags without leaving your library.

Privacy

Your music collection stays yours.

Vano Audio Player stores its library state and preferences locally on your Mac. It does not include advertising, tracking, or analytics. Optional lookups for lyrics, MusicBrainz metadata, Cover Art Archive artwork, and AcoustID identification are made only when you choose to use those features.
