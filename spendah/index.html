Spendah

Daily budget control from your monthly plan

Set your budget for the month and Spendah automatically splits it across all days. Example: if your monthly budget is 1000, your daily limit is about 32. Spendah helps you stay within that amount.

💸 How it works

Spendah gives you a clear daily limit based on your monthly budget, so you always know how much you can spend today without breaking your plan.

⚙️ Modes

Piggy Bank: strict daily cap. Today is fixed and you cannot spend more than today allows.

Carryover: flexible spending. You can borrow from tomorrow when needed and balance later.

Two spending styles for different habits

🎨 Themes and Apple Watch

Spendah supports multiple visual themes and also works on Apple Watch, so your budget is always on your wrist.
